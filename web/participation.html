arXiv Research

We invite you to participate in a research study about how people conduct searches in the arXiv database. This research will help us understand how people conduct searches and what may be most helpful in providing accurate information to users like you when they search this database.

This experimental interface is supported in part by the National Science Foundation, and hosted at Rutgers University during development and testing. For information about the project visit: http://arxiv_xs.rutgers.edu/


Please note that you must be at least 18 years of age to participate. Are you 18 or older?

     



Sorry, you cannot participate in this research.

The FAQ below will help you choose your level of participation.

♦ What is the benefit of this research?

This research might not benefit you directly, but it will help us better design search systems for users of databases, and the arXiv in particular. This means, users like you should benefit from it.

♦ Are there any risk of this research?

There are no foreseeable risks to participating in this research.

♦ How are the user's privacy and confidentiality of data insured?

If you decide to participate, we will do our best to protect your privacy as a research participant. Since the information is being sent (transmitted) over the Internet, there is a possibility that an outside party (such as a computer hacker) may be able to gain access to your information. If you use the arXiv on a computer in a publicly accessible area, such as in a restaurant, a school, a library, or at work, please understand that this can increase the possibility that others may be able to access your responses. Therefore, we strongly recommend you to conduct your search on a private computer to protect your privacy. Please remember to always completely close your browser (or log-off) after completing your search.

♦  How can I participate? Participation Levels Explained

You can participate in this research on three different levels:

Anonymous Participation Your search is completely anonymous.
Session-based Confidential Participation Your search is confidential. The software used to collect the data will not record the IP address of your computer or any other identifying information.
Login-based Confidential Participation Your search is confidential. The software used to collect the data will not record any identifying information except your log-in name. You may create multiple topic names if you have several distinct research interests.

♦ How else can I participate?

At the end of your search we will ask you for some feedback. Giving us this feedback is optional.

♦ How will the data be collected?

We will track your search information for as long as you conduct your current search. Typical searches take from 1 to 30 minutes. If you choose login-based participation, we will track your search data every time you search in the arXiv with your login name for the next 12 months. The research team and the Institutional Review Board (a committee that reviews research studies in order to protect research participants) at Rutgers University are the only parties that will be allowed to access the data, except as may be required by law. If a report of this study is published, or the results are presented at a professional conference, only group results will be stated. All study data will be kept for 6 years.

♦ Is my participation voluntary?

Your participation is entirely voluntary and you may choose not to participate in this study or withdraw your consent at any time by changing your participation status. You will not be penalized in any way should you choose not to participate or withdraw.
